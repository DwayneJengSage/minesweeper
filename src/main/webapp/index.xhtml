Welcome

This is a multiplayer minesweeper; every player shares the same board.
If you're alone, you can try it out by opening several tabs.

Instructions :
  • Enter your nickname and press Enter or click on "Connect".
  • Use the left click to open a cell and the right click to put a flag on it (represented by an orange cell).
  • Once you have flagged all the bombs around an open cell, you can quickly open the remaining adjacent cells by left-clicking on it.
  • Unlike traditional minesweeper, you have several lives. If you click on a bomb, a flag appears and you lose one life. You can see your life total below the board.
  • You can reset the game by clicking on the "Restart" button.

You can also use the chat to discuss with other players.

Good luck !
